Foster Inclusion,

Inspire Change.

The Akorede inclusion Foundation is a passionate organization committed to improving the quality of life for individuals living with disabilities, especially at the grassroots level. Our mission is to promote inclusion, advocate for rights, and support self-determination by empowering individuals to make informed choices, express their views, and participate in decision-making processes. We strive to foster social integration by ensuring access to education, employment, and community participation. Through advocacy, education, and awareness, we aim to combat stigma and discrimination while promoting positive attitudes toward disability. We support rural development programs to ensure equal access to opportunities in underserved areas and collaborate with partners to amplify our impact. Additionally, we provide financial and material support, conduct research to develop effective interventions, and offer essential rehabilitation services, including assistive technologies and mental health support. Our holistic approach ensures that no one is left behind in the journey toward dignity, inclusion, and empowerment.

We Aim to Achieve the Above Through;

Empowerment and Self-Determination

To enable individuals living with disabilities make informed choices, express their views, and participate in decision making process

Inclusion and Social Integration

To create a society where they are fully integrated into all aspects of life, including education, employment and community participation

Advocacy and Rights Protection

To advocate for their rights, freedom and interests through equal opportunities and access to resources facilities.

Awareness and Education

To raise public awareness about various disabilities, promote positive attitudes and combat stigma and discrimination.

Community Development and Rural Empowerment

To organise, guide, and support rural development programs, ensuring that individuals living with disabilities in rural areas have equal access to opportunities and services

Partnerships and Collaboration

To establish partnerships and collaborate with other organizations, government agencies and individuals to amplify the impact of the Foundation's work.

Financial and Material Support

To provide financial and material support to individuals living with disabilities and their families, ensuring access to essential needs and resources.

Research and Innovation

To conduct research and promote innovation to improve understanding and to develop effective interventions and strategies.

Rehabilitation and Support Services

To provide access to essential resources and support services, including assistive worktools, technologies and mental health support
